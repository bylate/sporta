About the Case Studies Branch

The Case Study branch of the Sports Analysis Club delves into the careers and statistics of individual players, offering in-depth analysis and insights into their performance and impact on the game.

Franz Wagner: The Versatile Forward Elevating the Orlando Magic

Summary: This case study examines Franz Wagner's impact on the Orlando Magic, highlighting his adaptability both on and off the ball, his playmaking skills, and his defensive capabilities, while acknowledging areas for improvement in finishing and three-point consistency to solidify his role as a key team asset alongside Paolo Banchero.

Bayer Leverkusen’s Unbeaten Run in the Bundesliga 23/24

Summary: Bayer Leverkusen's 2024 Bundesliga title win, led by head coach Xabi Alonso, marked a historic achievement, ending Bayern Munich's long-standing dominance with an unbeaten run, tactical innovation, and the emergence of a formidable new force in German football.
